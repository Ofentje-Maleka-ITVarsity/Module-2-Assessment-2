Turning Red

Mei Lee is a 13-year-old girl who suddenly “poofs” into a giant red panda when she gets too excited (which is practically always). Her mother, Ming, is protective, if not slightly overbearing, and never far from her daughter – an unfortunate reality for the coming-of-age teenager.
