Nando's

Nando's is a South African multinational
fast food chain that specialises in flame-grilled
peri-peri style chicken. Founded in Johannesburg
in 1987, Nando's operates over 1,200 outlets in
30 countries. Their logo depicts the Rooster of
Barcelos, one of the most common
symbols of Portugal.
