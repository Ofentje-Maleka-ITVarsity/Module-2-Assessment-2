Edgars Stores Limited is a retailer
offering clothing and merchandise.
The Company's segments include Edgars,
Jet, Manufacturing and Corporate.
Its core business is the retailing
of clothing, footwear, textiles and
accessories. The Company's business
is organized into two units:
manufacturing and retailing.
The Edgars segment provides shopping
for the middle to upper-income
groups. The Jet segment offers clothing,
as well as items sold by general dealers,
such as white goods, kitchenware,
hardware and a variety of other goods.
